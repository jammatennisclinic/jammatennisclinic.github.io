Sacramento Team

Hunter Tsai

Hunter leads the Sacramento Branch and serves as one of the head coaches for JAMMA Sacramento. With eight years of tennis experience, he is the captain and top singles player for the Folsom High School Varsity Tennis team. As a coach, Hunter is passionate about challenging his students to reach their full potential and seeing them improve. He is committed to creating a tennis environment where competition, learning, and enjoyment go hand in hand.

Lyla van Loben Sels

Lyla is a head coach for JAMMA Sacramento. She has been playing tennis since she was 5 and playing competitively since the age of 7, recently winning the 2023 CIF sectionals for her school. Lyla enjoys teaching both kids and adults who are passionate about the sport and wants to help them achieve their goals. She enjoys being a part of their growth in the sport. Outside of tennis, Lyla also has teaching and leadership skills from Girl Scouts and band.

Ambareesh Lankipalli

Ambareesh is one of the head coaches for the Sacramento Class. He has been playing tennis for over 7 years now and is the captain and #1 singles player of the Cosumnes Oaks Varsity Tennis Team. He is excited to foster a positive environment for all kids wanting to learn tennis.

Lily Guo

Lily is a junior at Folsom High School and has played both fourth and fifth singles on the Girls Varsity Tennis Team over the past couple of years. With two years of coaching experience and four years of playing, she is passionate about teaching students the fundamentals of tennis, providing constructive feedback while ensuring the experience is fun. Lily hopes to inspire growth in sportsmanship, competitiveness, and a love for the game. Off the court, she enjoys watching both tennis and basketball.

Claire Liu
JAMMA Sacramento

Claire is a coach for the Sacramento class. She started playing in elementary and is now a sophomore at Folsom High School, playing varsity #3 singles. Claire loves helping children grow and improve their abilities while encouraging them every step of the way. Besides tennis, she enjoys tutoring in dance and school subjects.
